Hotels in Mauritius

Enter your dates and choose from amazing stays.

Hotels

Le Meridien Mauritius

Le Meridien Mauritius is a prestigious beachfront resort offering an exquisite blend of elegance and comfort. Nestled along the golden shores of Pointe aux Piments, the hotel features refined suites, infinity pools, gourmet dining, and a world-class spa. Guests are treated to panoramic ocean views and impeccable service in a serene tropical paradise.

Long Beach Hotel

Long Beach Mauritius is a sophisticated luxury resort located on the pristine sands of Belle Mare. Renowned for its contemporary architecture and vibrant ambiance, the hotel offers lavish suites, fine dining experiences, and exclusive water sports. It is the perfect destination for travelers seeking both indulgence and adventure in an idyllic coastal setting.

Maritim Resort & Spa

Maritim Resort & Spa is a grand heritage estate surrounded by lush botanical gardens and a protected marine reserve. Combining colonial charm with modern luxury, the resort offers a private beach, elegant villas, championship golf facilities, and an award-winning spa. It is an oasis of tranquility and timeless sophistication.

The Oberoi Mauritius

The Oberoi Mauritius is an iconic symbol of refined luxury, offering private villas and pavilions set within tropical gardens overlooking Turtle Bay. With personalized butler service, gourmet cuisine, and holistic wellness experiences, this exclusive retreat promises unparalleled privacy and serenity for the most discerning guests.

Lux Grand Baie

Lux Grand Baie redefines modern luxury with its chic design, rooftop infinity pools, and premium lifestyle experiences. Located in the heart of Grand Baie, the resort offers stylish suites, world-class restaurants, and an innovative wellness spa. It is a glamorous sanctuary for travelers who appreciate sophistication and vibrant coastal living.

Sofitel L’Imperial Resort & Spa

Sofitel L’Imperial is a masterpiece of French elegance infused with Asian-inspired serenity. Set against the dramatic sunsets of Flic en Flac, the resort offers opulent rooms, landscaped gardens, and indulgent spa rituals . Guests enjoy an atmosphere of refined relaxation and exceptional hospitality by the Indian Ocean.

Ambre Resort

Ambre Resort is a luxurious adults-only haven set along the crystal-clear lagoon of Belle Mare. This all-inclusive resort offers stylish beachfront rooms, gourmet dining, and exclusive leisure experiences. With its tranquil ambiance and vibrant entertainment, Ambre creates the perfect balance between romance and refined pleasure.

Tamassa Bel Ombre

Tamassa Bel Ombre is a contemporary luxury resort surrounded by breathtaking mountain landscapes and sugarcane fields. Known for its vibrant elegance and warm hospitality, the resort features spacious rooms, cascading pools, and an indulgent spa. It delivers a unique fusion of relaxation, style, and tropical charm.
